Fellowship Materials

PhD fellowship applications are extremely nebulous and difficult. When applying for fellowships, I found a lack of publicly available examples for HCI / information science. To help others in the process, I wanted to list my 2020/2021 application materials for three of my successful fellowship attempts. They are very similar, just tailored to the requirements of each fellowship. Note that I applied to all three of these fellowships in the 2019/2020 cycle and was rejected, though my proposal was rather similar.

I have also written a Medium Blog Post on the process of applying and interviewing for these fellowships.
